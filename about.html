Company Overview

Founded in 1997 by Mr. Nanding A. Bancoro and Engr. Marieta A. Bancoro in Cabuyao, Laguna, REIGN-NAN Sales Industry & General Contractor, Inc. has grown from a single proprietorship into a reputable corporation providing high-quality products and services for industrial and commercial applications. With over two decades of experience, REIGN-NAN is now a trusted sourcing company, supplier of mechanical seals, and provider of technical services tailored to the unique needs of its clients.

MISSION
In order to realize our Vision, we are committed to exceed the expectations of our customers, whom we define as partners, and fellow employees. We will accomplish this by working closely with our customers to maximize efficiency in the procurement process resulting to cost effectiveness in order to yield maximum return which benefits we intend to share with our customers thereby enhancing our competitiveness from the price perspective. In this way we will ensure that our profit, quality and growth goals are met.
VISION
