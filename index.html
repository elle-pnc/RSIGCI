Company Overview

Founded in 1997 by Mr. Nanding A. Bancoro and Engr. Marieta A. Bancoro in Cabuyao, Laguna, REIGN-NAN Sales Industry & General Contractor, Inc. (RSGCI) has grown from a single proprietorship into a reputable corporation committed to providing high-quality products and services for industrial and commercial applications.

With over two decades of experience in the industry, RSGCI is now a trusted sourcing company, supplier of mechanical seals, and provider of technical services tailored to the unique needs of its clients.

Originally known as REIGN-NAN Sales Industry, the company officially became a corporation on September 26, 2008, reflecting its rapid growth and diversification. Registered under SEC No. CS200815149, the company continues to thrive through its core operations in sourcing, trading, installation, commissioning, industrial painting, and cladding works for steam pipelines.

Leadership & Team

Presided by Mr. Nanding A. Bancoro and managed by Engr. Marieta A. Bancoro, the company is composed of highly trained and experienced personnel committed to customer satisfaction. Our team works closely with clients to understand their needs and recommend the most suitable products and services.
